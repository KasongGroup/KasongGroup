Who We Are

SUTCOS inatoa mafunzo ya ujuzi katika fani mbalimbali, mafunzo haya yatakufanya uwe na uwezo wa kushindana kwenye soko la ajira pamoja na kujiajiri. Pia tunatoa kozi ya kifaransa, kiingereza na matumizi ya kompyuta bure ili kupanua wigo wa ushindani wa wanafunzi wetu katika soko la ajira. Lengo letu ni kufikisha elimu bora kwa umma wa Watanzania kwa gharama nafuu zaidi.

Course programs

We offer various courses which will give you a competitive advantage over others in the job industry. In addition, our courses are structured in such a way that graduates are ready for self-employment. After emnrollment our students get chance to learn other languages such as French, English and Computer application for free hence bringing them closer to the ditigal transformation.
